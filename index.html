ABOUT CONVERSE

Browse the profiles of other users with different opinions. You can choose to send them an invite or simply try to match with them. Once both parties accept, discuss!
While discussing, view a sidebar of helpful information and recommended topics, to really get the discussion going!
When you're done talking, or if you've been having a really great conversation, rate it! Are they a great conversationalist? Let them know! Build reputation by having good conversation. The better your reputation, the more people will want to converse!
